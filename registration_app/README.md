What This Is

A simple, professional online registration form for collecting candidate applications.
Candidates fill in their details on one page, and once submitted, they see a
confirmation page with everything they entered — while you receive the complete
submission on WhatsApp automatically, ready to review.

No paperwork, no manual data entry, no missed applications.


How It Works

Step 1 — The Candidate Fills the Form

Candidates land on a clean, guided registration form and enter:


Full Name, Mobile Number, Email Address
Address, City, State, PIN Code
Gender, Date of Birth
Education, Position Applied For, Skills, Experience
Resume (PDF)
Profile Photo


Every field is checked as they type, so mistakes get caught immediately — for example,
a phone number with the wrong number of digits, a missing @ in the email, or an
attempt to upload the wrong file type. This means the information you receive is
always complete and correctly formatted, with no back-and-forth needed to fix errors.

Step 2 — Instant Confirmation

Once submitted, the candidate is shown a confirmation page with a summary of everything
they entered, so they know their application went through. From there they can also go
back and edit any detail if they made a mistake.

Step 3 — You Get Notified on WhatsApp, Automatically

The moment a candidate submits the form, their complete application — every field,
neatly organized — is sent straight to WhatsApp, addressed to your number. No need to
log into any dashboard or check email; the details land in your WhatsApp chat, ready to
read, forward, or act on.

A typical message looks like this:


NEW CANDIDATE REGISTRATION
Full Name, Mobile, Email, Gender, Date of Birth
Address, City, State, PIN Code
Position Applied For, Education, Skills, Experience
Links to view the uploaded Resume and Photo
Submission ID and timestamp




Built-In Safeguards


No incomplete submissions — every required field must be filled in correctly
before the form can be submitted.
No duplicate entries — if a candidate accidentally double-clicks Submit, or hits
Refresh, the system prevents a second copy of the same application from being
created.
Only real resumes and photos are accepted — the system checks that an uploaded
resume is genuinely a PDF and that a photo is genuinely an image, not just that the
filename looks right.
All submissions are securely stored, and every application can be reviewed at any
time.



Setting Your WhatsApp Number

The WhatsApp number that receives every submission is set once, in a single place, by
whoever manages the system for you. If you ever need to change the number the
notifications go to, just let your developer/support contact know the new number and
they can update it in a couple of minutes.


Need Something Changed or Added?

This system can be adjusted to fit your needs — for example, adding or removing form
fields, changing the look and colors, sending notifications to multiple numbers, or
connecting it to an email or spreadsheet as well. Just share what you'd like changed
with your developer/support contact.
